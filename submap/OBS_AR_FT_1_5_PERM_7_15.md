**Title:** (AIS) The Argument of Mark, S. Baylis

The Argument of Mark by S. Baylis presents a theological interpretation of the Gospel of Mark, emphasizing Jesus as the fulfillment of Old Testament prophecies and the establishment of a new community empowered by the Spirit. The narrative arc illustrates Jesus' authority through His actions and teachings, the conflict with Jewish leaders, and the formation of a remnant that carries forward His message. The climax highlights the significance of Jesus' death and resurrection, which inaugurates a new mission for His disciples. The work also addresses eschatological themes, linking them to the broader narrative of salvation and the continuation of Jesus' authority through His followers.
